The rating system:



The Environmental Working Group's Skin Deep Database rates products based on the ingredient hazard score . Score 1-10 (1 is best) reflects known and suspected hazards of ingredients. We display only EWG verified (Best: All ingredients declared, all are safe), Rating (Lowest Hazard) and Rating (Low Hazard), because we think only low to no-hazard products are worth considering. Then we sort them by price. Touch the certification button next to the product to find more ingredient information on the EWG site.
